Before gliding, make sure you are physically and mentally prepared. Make use of a wind current or elevated terrain for take off. Keep an eye on the wind conditions while gliding to maintain steady balance. Pay attention to air traffic conditions and only use a wind glider while the date on your gliding license remains valid. Keep your balance while gliding and retract the wind glider at a safe altitude. Don't drink and glide!
